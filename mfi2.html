Gaiaonline

This is a community website. In this website you can experience making your own avatar. To get gold(money) you play mini games, daily chances, and clicking on trees.If you recieved an item you don't want you can sell it on the gaia market. The market will take a 3% tax from the gold you recieve from a person buying your item. You can go to town get a house and car and design it with any peices you recieve or buy.
